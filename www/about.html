About Boardytale

Boardytale is hexagonal&pixelart webgame deeply inspired by Battle for Wesnoth. Boardytale is focused on a lot of little swift multiplayer games with its own tales. Project is currently in deeply alfa state.

Boardytale project is managed by Zdeněk Mlčoch - @Thiltal.
